VI. Combat Phase
-----------------
Flaminius PC attacks 2nd Illyrian War with 5Reg, 1Vet (III), and 2Fle
2nd Illyrian War (4/2 D5/S17) [10t spoils]
Flaminius PC has a strength of 7 + 4 = 11
Combat bonus is 11 - 4 = +7
Rolls a 10 + 7 = 17; Victory! 1 Legion (V) & 1 Fleet (II) lost!
Flaminius gains 2 Inf and 2 Pop; Legion IV now veteran.

Cunctator FC attacks 1st Gallic War with 12Reg and 2Vet (I,II)
1st Gallic War (10/0 D13/S15) [20t spoils]
Cunctator FC has a strength of 16 + 5 = 21
Combat bonus is 21 - 10 = +11
Rolls a 9 + 11 = 20; Victory! No losses!
Cunctator gains 5 Inf and 5 Pop; Legion IX now veteran.
Province of Cisalpine Gaul created.

Note: According to the PBEM Rules version 4.1, "provinces aren't attacked by 
wars on the turn they are created. So, if the Gallic War is defeated, thereby
creating Cisalpine Gaul, the 2nd Punic War (if active) won't attack the
province until the following turn."  [See Romulus game rules, item #5, PBEM 
rules by Roberto Ullfig, as given at Joan's Republic of Rome page.]
