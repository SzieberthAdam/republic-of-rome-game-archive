Event

Enemy Leader Dies

Any one enemy Leader in play at the end of the Forum Phase of the HRAO's choice is discarded. Not applicable to Rebel Senators.

Remove at start of next Forum Phase.

Enemy Sues for Peace

Dishearted by the loss of their Leader. the largest current matching War of that Leader sues for peace. The State receives half of any Spoils of War (fractions rounded down) and shuffles the War card back into the deck. Not applicable to Rebel Senators.

Remove at start of next Forum Phase.
