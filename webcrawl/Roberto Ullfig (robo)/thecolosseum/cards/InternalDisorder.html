Event

Internal Disorder

All undeveloped Provinces lose all Revenue during the coming turn and may not attempt Improvement dr. Next Revenue Phase: Revolt DR <= 4 = Revolt. Garrison Legion = +1 DRM per legion. Revolt = Governor and Legions killed. Province becomes War card with Provincial Forces.

Remove at start of next Forum Phase.

Remove at start of next Forum Phase.
