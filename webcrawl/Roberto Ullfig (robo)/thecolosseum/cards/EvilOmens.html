Event

Evil Omens

State (or Pontifex Maximus) pays 20 Talents immediately. -1 to all die/dice rolls except +1 to normal Persuasion Attempts. No effect to Initiative DR.

Remove at start of next Forum Phase.

More Evil Omens

Confidence erodes at all levels of government. Pontifex Maximus deposed with loss of 5 Influence. -2 to all die/dice rolls except +2 to Persuasion Attempts. No effect to Initiative DR. More Evil Omens have cumulative effect.

Remove at start of next Forum Phase.
