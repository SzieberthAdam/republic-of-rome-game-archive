Ariobarzanes III : King of Cappadocia 51-42 BC

Playable during the Revolution Phase in which a Governor of Bithynia-Pontus declares himself as a Primary or Secondary Rebel. Rebel gains 20 talents and 5 provincial armies are added to Bithynia-Pontus. Increase Bithynia-Pontus' Maximum Force by 5 armies while the rebellion is ongoing.
